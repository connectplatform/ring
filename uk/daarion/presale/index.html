Introduction‚Äã

DAARION is an innovative Ukrainian agri-tech startup building a next-generation decentralized cooperative through the Greenfood.live platform. With a focus on Web 3.0 and blockchain, DAARION aims to establish a sustainable economic framework that empowers small-scale farmers and producers of organic goods while also benefiting the local economy.

Key Challenges‚Äã

Small-scale farmers face challenges such as limited access to markets, funding, and resources. DAARION uses blockchain-powered solutions to address these issues, promoting transparency and fostering a closed-loop economic system through robust partnerships between producers and consumers.

Token Distribution‚Äã

  • Token Issuance: Tokens are distributed directly to wallets after confirmation of payment.
  • Vesting Schedule: Core Team tokens are subject to a 12-month vesting schedule, ensuring long-term commitment to project success.

Token Utility‚Äã

The DAAR token is integrated into the Greenfood.live platform to provide real-world utility:

  1. Product Exchange: Exchange tokens for organic products such as honey, amaranth, and other agricultural goods.
  2. Governance: Participate in important decisions about the future direction of the Greenfood.live ecosystem.
  3. Staking Rewards: Earn additional DAAR tokens by staking.
  4. Bonuses: Gain unique bonuses and rewards by participating in the DAARION ecosystem.

Note: 1 DAAR = 1 liter of premium honey (lifetime exchange rate).

Buying DAAR Tokens‚Äã

How to Buy‚Äã

You can participate in the DAAR presale by following these steps:

  1. Add DAAR to MetaMask:
    • Open MetaMask and select "Add Token".
    • Insert the token contract address and confirm.
  2. Invest:
    • Access the presale dashboard on Greenfood.live.
    • Choose the number of DAAR tokens you want to purchase.
    • Confirm the transaction via your crypto wallet.

Payment Methods‚Äã

The following payment methods are supported:

  • ETH (Ethereum)
  • USDT (Tether)
  • Direct Bank Transfer

Note: Ensure the correct wallet address is entered before payment to avoid transaction issues.

Listing on QuickSwap‚Äã

Post-presale, DAAR tokens will be listed on QuickSwap to provide liquidity for trading.
