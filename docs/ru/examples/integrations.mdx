# Third-party Integrations

Integration examples with popular services including CRM systems, payment gateways, and communication tools.

## 🔗 Popular Integrations

### CRM Integration
- **Salesforce** - Sync entities and opportunities
- **HubSpot** - Lead management and tracking
- **Pipedrive** - Sales pipeline integration

### Payment Gateways
- **Stripe** - Global payment processing
- **PayPal** - Alternative payment method
- **WayForPay** - Ukrainian market payments

### Communication Tools
- **Slack** - Team notifications and updates
- **Discord** - Community integration
- **Microsoft Teams** - Enterprise communication
