# Opportunities System

Dual-nature marketplace supporting both **offers** and **requests** across 7 opportunity types with confidential access tiers.

## Overview

Ring Platform's opportunity system revolutionizes how organizations connect by supporting both what they offer and what they need.

## Dual-Nature System

### Offers
What your entity provides:
- Job positions
- Partnership opportunities
- Resources and services
- Event hosting
- Mentorship programs

### Requests  
What your entity needs:
- Talent acquisition
- Partnership seeking
- Resource requirements
- Service needs
- Expertise requests

## 7 Opportunity Types

### 1. Job Opportunities
- Full-time positions
- Part-time roles
- Contract work
- Internships

### 2. Partnership Opportunities
- Strategic alliances
- Joint ventures
- Collaboration projects
- Business partnerships

### 3. Volunteer Opportunities
- Non-profit work
- Community service
- Pro bono projects
- Social impact initiatives

### 4. Mentorship Opportunities
- Professional mentoring
- Industry guidance
- Skill development
- Career coaching

### 5. Resource Opportunities
- Equipment sharing
- Space rental
- Service exchange
- Knowledge sharing

### 6. Event Opportunities
- Conference speaking
- Workshop hosting
- Networking events
- Training sessions

### 7. Custom Opportunities
- Specialized needs
- Industry-specific opportunities
- Unique collaborations

## Access Tiers

### Public Opportunities
- Visible to all platform users
- Standard application process
- Open competition

### Confidential Opportunities
- Restricted to CONFIDENTIAL role users
- Private application process
- Exclusive access
