Ring is the world's most comprehensive free open-source platform for solving human needs collectively with AI orchestration. Whether you're building a marketplace for artisans, an opportunities network for cooperatives, or a custom platform for your community's specific needs - Ring provides everything you need.

## Why Choose Ring for White-label?

**Complete Business Platform**: 20+ integrated modules including entities, opportunities, store, wallet, NFT market, staking, messaging, notifications, and more.

**AI-Web3 Symbiosis**: AI agents analyze and match opportunities automatically, while Web3 ensures ownership and token economics align incentives.

**Own Your Economy**: Deploy with your own RING token or integrate existing tokens - your platform runs on your rules.

**Database Flexibility**: Choose from Firebase, PostgreSQL, or ConnectPlatform backend - migrate anytime without rewriting code.

**Global Scale Ready**: Multi-tenant architecture supports serving thousands of organizations from a single deployment.
