## Component Architecture Principles

### Separation of Concerns & Hierarchy
Ring Platform establishes modern component architecture patterns that ensure maintainability, reusability, and proper separation of concerns.

<Mermaid>
{`flowchart TD
    A[Hooks Layer] --> B[Feature Components]
    B --> C[Page Components]
    D[Data Access] --> A
    E[UI Primitives] --> B

    A[useCreditBalance<br/>useAuth<br/>useRealtime]
    B[WalletSection<br/>KYCUpload<br/>MobileAvatarButton]
    C[Profile Page<br/>Wallet Page<br/>Dashboard]`}
</Mermaid>
